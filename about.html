Biography

    Outcome of Betrayal is a metal band based in Saint Louis, Missouri. The band had its beginnings in 2008. Guitarist Brian Crangle and vocalist James Simpson, friends from prior music ventures, decided to start their own group. The next year, in 2009, Patrick Sommer was added to the band as the drummer and things really started to come together. After months of searching Jarrod Meine was added as the second guitarist, followed by Mark Drago as the bands bass player.

    By June of 2010, the band had composed music and perfected a full set list. It was at this same time that they recorded a demo CD of 4 songs. Not long after the demo was released the band decided to move forward without Jarrod Meine. Almost immediately Alex Hughes stepped in and brought a new addition to the group. The band continued to write new music and continued playing shows in the St Louis area.

    They quickly gained momentum and fans in the local St Louis music scene as well as online. This included winning a spot at "Pointfest" (a music festival put on by 105.7 The Point, a local radio station) in the summer of 2012 by winning a multi level competition called "Battle for Pointfest". This allowed the band to play in front of it's largest crowd ever, gaining a lot of exposure.

    In the late Summer of 2012, Bass Player Mark Drago and founding guitarist Brian Crangle decided to part ways from the band due to personal reasons. The remaining members decided to go on hiatus for the remainder of the yearto continue writing while finding replacements. A friend of the band Alex Schuler decided to step up and try out to fill the guitar slot. He quickly added a new formula to the bands writing structure and became the new guitarist in the late summer of 2012. In the fall of 2012 the band took on friend Danny Wesche as their new bass player after he came in and picked up the spot like it was his own. Alex and Danny have brought a new light and spirit to the band, and have made Outcome of Betrayal stronger than ever.

    On October 31st 2012, the band released its official 4 song EP called "It's Time to Change" nationwide on major online media outlets and stores like I-tunes & Amazon.

    Outcome of Betrayal has been influenced by a number of well-known metal bands. They include Soilwork, Killswitch Engage, As I Lay Dying, Haste the Day, and In Flames. The band took inspiration from these groups and learned from each of their skills and styles. In the end, the band has created a unique style with fast and heavy instrumentals and a wide range of vocal techniques and sounds.
